## 2. Trend Analysis (Top 3)

### Trend 1: The "Post-Caffeine" Movement
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What's Happening** | Gen Z and Millennials are quitting high-dose caffeine (300mg+) due to anxiety/cortisol concerns. |
| **Evidence** | Rise of "Decaf" searches (+30% YoY) and "Cortisol Mocktails". |
| **Opportunity** | Position Brewy not as *anti-caffeine*, but as **"Optimized Caffeine"** (Caffeine + L-Theanine + Lion's Mane). |

### Trend 2: "Clinical Receipts" (Radical Transparency)
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What's Happening** | Consumers are demanding COAs (Certificates of Analysis) because of heavy metal scares in powders. |
| **Who's Leading** | Nootropics Depot, Momentous. |
| **Opportunity** | **Be the only coffee brand that puts the lab report on the bag.** |

### Trend 3: "Ritual Over Rush"
| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **What's Happening** | The "Grindset" (Hustle Culture) is dead. "Soft Life" and "Flow State" are in. |
| **Evidence** | "Slow Morning" trend on TikTok (2B views). |
| **Opportunity** | Marketing should focus on **Calm Focus**, not "crushing it". |

## 8. What This Means for Brewy

### 8.1 Industry-Specific Power Moves

#### Power Move 1: The "Open Source" Formula
- **What**: Publish the exact mg of Lion's Mane and Caffeine per cup.
- **Why Now**: Competitors use "Proprietary Blends" to hide under-dosing.
- **How**: "We don't have a secret formula. Here it is."
- **Impact**: Instant trust from the skeptical "Creative Class".

#### Power Move 2: The "Anti-Mud" Campaign
- **What**: Direct head-to-head taste test with MudWtr/Ryze.
- **Why Now**: People are buying mushroom coffee but *hating* the taste.
- **How**: Send a "Taste Guarantee" kit. "If you don't like it, we buy you a MudWtr."
- **Impact**: High viral potential. Aggressive but confident.
