# Company Brief: Brewy

## Overview
**Brewy** is a modern, functional beverage brand specializing in mushroom-infused coffee and teas. The brand focuses on natural energy, mental clarity, and immune support without the jitters of traditional caffeine.

## Industry / Niche
Functional Beverages / Mushroom Coffee / Health & Wellness

## Target Audience
- Health-conscious professionals
- Bio-hackers and productivity seekers
- Coffee lovers looking for a healthier alternative
- Wellness enthusiasts interested in adaptogens

## Vibe / Aesthetic
- **Keywords**: Natural, Earthy, Modern, clean, Organic, Flow, Clarity.
- **Visual Style**: Minimalist packaging, soft earth tones (browns, greens, creams), high-quality photography, approachable yet premium.
- **Tone**: Educated, friendly, empowering, grounded.

## Goals
- Establish Brewy as a premium yet accessible entry point into functional mushrooms.
- Build a strong community around "mindful energy".
- Dominate social media with visually appealing, educational content.
