## 2. Meta Ads Strategy (Facebook & Instagram)

### Funnel Structure

#### Top of Funnel (TOF) - Prospecting lists
*   **Audiences**:
    *   *Interest Stack*: "Huberman Lab", "Tim Ferriss", "Notion", "Figma", "Biohacking".
    *   *Lookalike*: 1% of Buyers (once data permits). In start: Broad targeting with creative filtering.
*   **Creative Concept A: "The Switch" (Us vs. Them)**
    *   **Visual**: Split screen video.
    *   *Left*: "Old Coffee" (Jitters, Anxiety, 2pm Crash). Color grade: Grimy/Blue.
    *   *Right*: "Brewy" (Flow, Calm, Focus). Color grade: Warm/Sharp.
    *   **Headline**: "Stop borrowing energy from tomorrow."
    *   **CTA**: Shop Now.
*   **Creative Concept B: "The Lab Report" (Static)**
    *   **Visual**: High-res photo of the bag with the COA (Cert of Analysis) overlaid.
    *   **Text**: "500mg Lion's Mane. 0mg Heavy Metals. Verified."
    *   **Headline**: "The most transparent coffee in the world."

#### Middle of Funnel (MOF) - Engagement Retargeting
*   **Audience**: Engaged with IG/FB content (30 days) but didn't click.
*   **Creative**: "The Founder Story" (Video).
    *   **Hook**: "I built this because I kept burning out."
    *   **Angle**: Vulnerability = Trust.

#### Bottom of Funnel (BOF) - Website Visitors
*   **Audience**: Added to Cart (30 days) / View Content (14 days).
*   **Creative**: "The Guarantee" (Static).
    *   **Visual**: Simple text on brand background.
    *   **Copy**: "Don't love the flow? We'll buy you a bag of your old coffee. Zero risk."
    *   **Offer**: "Finish your checkout for free shipping."

## 4. TikTok Strategy

### "Spark Ads" Protocol
Do not run "Ads". Run "Content".
1.  **Seed the Product**: Send Brewy to 20 "Cozy Desk" creators.
2.  **Wait for Organic Hits**: If a creator's video gets >1k views organically, ask for the "Spark Code".
3.  **Put Money Behind It**: Boost THAT video from THEIR handle.
    *   *Why*: It looks authentic. It leverages their trust.
    *   *Budget*: $50/day per winning video.
