# Brewy: 14-Day Launch Sequence

## Phase 1: The Tease (Days 1-5)
**Goal**: Build the waitlist. Create mystery.

### Day 1: The Glitch (Static)
*   **Visual**: A coffee cup pixelating/glitching out. Dark, moody lighting.
*   **Caption**: "Your morning has a bug. The crash. The anxiety. The jitters. We found the patch. 01.01.26."
*   **Prompt**: `Midjourney: /imagine prompt: a ceramic coffee cup shifting reality, digital glitch effect, forest green and mocha colors, cinematic lighting --ar 4:5`

### Day 2: The Definition (Text)
*   **Visual**: Dictionary definition style.
*   **Text**: "Functional (adj): Designed to be practical and useful, rather than attractive." -> "Brewy (noun): Both."
*   **Caption**: "Why choose between science and art? Waitlist in bio."

### Day 3: The Enemy (Reel)
*   **Visual**: Fast cuts of someone stressed, spilling coffee, shaking hands.
*   **Audio**: Tense violins -> sudden silence -> bird sounds.
*   **Text Overlay**: "Coffee wasn't meant to hurt you."
*   **Script**: "You drink it to wake up. But it makes you anxious. You drink it to focus. But it makes you jittery. Stop the cycle. Reset."

### Day 4: The Spore (Macro Photo)
*   **Visual**: Extreme close up of a Lion's Mane mushroom. Texture looks like a brain.
*   **Caption**: "This is Lion's Mane. It boosts Nerve Growth Factor. It's not magic. It's biology. And it's in your morning brew."

### Day 5: The Countdown (Motion)
*   **Visual**: A sun rising over a misty forest. Text "24 Hours."
*   **Caption**: "Tomorrow, we upgrade the bean. Be ready."

## Phase 2: The Reveal (Days 6-7)
**Goal**: Sales.

### Day 6: The Manifesto (Highlight Reel)
*   **Visual**: Beautiful montage. Pouring water, blooming beans, drinking, smiling, working.
*   **Script**: "We love coffee. But we hated the crash. So we didn't just make an alternative. We made an upgrade. Premium Arabica beans. Infused with 500mg of Lion's Mane. For focus that flows, not forces. This is Brewy. The Scientific Naturalist."
*   **CTA**: "The Beta Batch is live. Link in bio."

### Day 7: The Unboxing (Reel)
*   **Visual**: Hands opening the premium box. Showing the bag, the spoon, the whisk.
*   **Script**: "Inside the kit. You get the bag. The matte black whisk. And the dosing spoon. Everything you need for a better brain."
